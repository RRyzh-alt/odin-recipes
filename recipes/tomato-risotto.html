Tomato Risotto Recipe

Description

This delicious tomato risotto will make a wholesome addition to your dinner table. Full of tomatoey goodness and tasty parmesan cheese, this is a healthy and flavourful dish, that is easy to make and even more pleasing to eat!

Ingredients

  • 400g risotto rice
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves chopped
  • rosemary
  • basil
  • salt
  • black pepper ground
  • half bag grated parmesan
  • cherry tomatoes handful
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Method

  1. Heat pan, add oil, saute onions and garlic on low for 5 minutes until cooked
  2. Add risotto rice to pan and toast for 5-10 minutes
  3. Add stock and enough water to cover rice and simmer
  4. Add tomato pureee, cherry tomatoes, black pepper, basil, rosemary and salt and cook on low until water is absorbed fully and rice is cooked al dente
  5. Add parmesan and cover for 5-10 minutes after removing from heat

serve and enjoy!
